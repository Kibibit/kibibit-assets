Market Research Survey Results

Current snapshot based on 39 usable responses from 56 raw records, including screenouts and incompletes.

Executive Summary

The clearest signal from this survey is that teams have already adopted AI, but most have not operationalized it. Respondents report fragmented AI usage across chats, local sessions, and tools; repeated prompts and duplicated context; weak measurement of impact; and rising pressure from leadership to justify spend and outcomes.

The strongest demand is not for more AI access, but for a system that enables visibility, comparison, reuse, auditability, and coordination. That aligns directly with Shepherd’s product thesis.
